myBlue


myBlue is Blue Cross & Blue Shield of Mississippi's primary mobile application for all covered members. Last year, I teamed up with several talented individuals within the Creative Team as well as the Technology and Development team to implement a redesign to the existing application. We conceptualized, wireframed and mocked-up interfaces and experiences based on high levels of member and user research by implementing best practices for design and accessibility standards.
